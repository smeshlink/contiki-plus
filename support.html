科学论文

Contiki 内部实现的许多机制都已经作为论文发表在计算机科学刊物上，这些论文提供了 Contiki 系统机制的技术细节和背后原因。

Contiki 中的低功耗IP网络

  • The ContikiMAC Radio Duty Cycling Protocol. Adam Dunkels. Technical report 2011.
  • Lossy Links, Low Power, High Throughput. Simon Duquennoy, Fredrik Österlind, and Adam Dunkels. ACM SenSys 2011.
  • Beyond Interoperability: Pushing the Performance of Sensornet IP Stacks. JeongGil Ko, Joakim Eriksson, Nicolas Tsiftes, Stephen Dawson-Haggerty, Mathilde Durvy, JP Vasseur, Andreas Terzis, Adam Dunkels, and David Culler. ACM SenSys 2011
  • A Low-power CoAP for Contiki. Matthias Kovatsch, Simon Duquennoy, and Adam Dunkels. IEEE IoTech 2011.
  • Efficient Application Integration in IP-based Sensor Networks. Dogan Yazar and Adam Dunkels. ACM BuildSys 2009.
  • Making Sensor Networks IPv6 Ready. Mathilde Durvy, Julien Abeillé, Patrick Wetterwald, Colin O'Flynn, Blake Leverett, Eric Gnoske, Michael Vidales, Geoff Mulligan, Nicolas Tsiftes, Niclas Finne, and Adam Dunkels. ACM SenSys 2008.
  • Making TCP/IP Viable for Wireless Sensor Networks. Adam Dunkels, Thiemo Voigt, and Juan Alonso. EWSN 2004.
  • Full TCP/IP for 8 Bit Architectures. Adam Dunkels. MobiSys 2003.

Contiki 机制

  • Enabling Large-Scale Storage in Sensor Networks with the Coffee File System. Nicolas Tsiftes, Adam Dunkels, Zhitao He, and Thiemo Voigt. IPSN 2009.

  • Run-time dynamic linking for reprogramming wireless sensor networks. Adam Dunkels, Niclas Finne, Joakim Eriksson, and Thiemo Voigt. ACM SenSys 2006.
  • Software-based on-line energy estimation for sensor nodes.. Adam Dunkels, Fredrik Österlind, Nicolas Tsiftes, and Zhitao He. EmNets 2007.
  • Protothreads: Simplifying event-driven programming of memory-constrained embedded systems. Adam Dunkels, Oliver Schmidt, Thiemo Voigt, and Muneeb Ali. ACM SenSys 2006.
